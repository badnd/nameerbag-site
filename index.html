Light Custom vs Full Custom

Use this section to guide customers to the right inquiry type.

Light Customization

  • Use existing product styles
  • Add logo, label, zipper puller or package
  • Lower MOQ and faster delivery
  • Best for promotional gifts and events

Full Customization

  • Develop new shape and structure
  • Customize material, size, strap, buckle and compartments
  • Private label and retail packaging support
  • Best for brands and long-term product programs
