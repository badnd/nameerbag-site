Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Tianjin Junyi Premium Trading Co.,Ltd. ("we", "us" or "our") respects the privacy and commercial confidentiality of buyers, suppliers and website visitors. This policy explains how we handle information received through this website, email, WhatsApp and other direct business communications.

1. Information We May Receive

When you contact us, you may provide your name, business email address, company name, telephone or messaging details, product interests, order requirements, artwork, specifications and other information relevant to a quotation or business relationship.

Our hosting and security providers may also process limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, requested pages and security logs to deliver and protect the website.

2. How We Use Information

  • To answer inquiries and prepare product quotations.
  • To discuss samples, customization, production, shipping and after-sales matters.
  • To maintain business records and comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect the website, our communications and our business from misuse or fraud.

3. Legal Basis

Where applicable, we process information to respond to your request, take steps toward a business contract, perform a contract, comply with legal obligations, or pursue legitimate business and security interests. Where consent is legally required, we will request it separately.

4. Sharing and International Processing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only when reasonably necessary with service providers, logistics partners, payment or professional advisers, authorities when legally required, or parties involved in fulfilling your business request. Because we serve international buyers, information may be processed outside your country. We take reasonable steps to protect it during such processing.

5. Retention and Security

We retain inquiry and business information only for as long as reasonably necessary for quotations, order follow-up, legal compliance and legitimate business records. We use reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect information, but no internet transmission or storage method can be guaranteed completely secure.

6. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction or objection regarding your personal information. You may also withdraw consent where processing relies on consent. These rights may be subject to legal limitations and record-keeping requirements.

7. Third-Party Communication Services

If you contact us through WhatsApp, email or another third-party service, that provider processes information under its own privacy terms. We recommend reviewing the relevant provider's policy.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy when our website, services or legal obligations change. The effective date shown above identifies the current version.
