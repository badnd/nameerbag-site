Company Profile

Tianjin Junyi Premium Trading Co.,Ltd. (天津俊逸优品贸易有限公司) was established with a clear mission: to provide global brands with premium, custom-manufactured bags at competitive factory-direct prices. Headquartered in Tianjin, one of China's major manufacturing and logistics hubs, our company has grown steadily over the past decade to become a trusted partner for businesses across North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and beyond.

Our 5000+ square-meter production facility is equipped with state-of-the-art cutting machines, high-speed sewing lines, screen printing stations, heat transfer equipment, and dedicated quality inspection zones. With a workforce of over 50 skilled technicians and QC specialists, we maintain rigorous standards throughout every stage of production — from raw material sourcing to final packaging.

We are proud to hold internationally recognized certifications including ISO 9001:2000, BSCI Verified Supplier status, SGS product testing, and RoHS compliance. These credentials reflect our unwavering commitment to quality management, ethical manufacturing practices, and environmental responsibility. Our monthly production capacity exceeds 50,000 units, enabling us to handle both small-batch custom orders and large-volume bulk shipments with equal efficiency.
