Frequently Asked Questions

Click on any question to see the answer

Can you add my logo on the bag(backpack)?+
Yes, we can. There are customization options of silk printing, sublimation, embroidery, pyrograph, rubber patch etc. Please send your logo to us and we will suggest the best way.
What is your shipping time?+
We have enough stock. We can deliver the goods as soon as we receive your payment. For custom OEM orders, production lead time is typically 10-20 days after sample approval depending on order quantity.
I am a new entrepreneur, can I order a small quantity to start with?+
Yes of course. Our MOQ is only 100 pcs with best price to support new business. We welcome startups and small businesses to work with us.
When can I get the tracking number?+
After we get your payment, we will deliver the goods to warehouse by domestic express. We can get the tracking number after the warehouse gets the goods. Usually it will take about 2-3 days.
How is your quality guarantee?+
We have QC department to examine each and every single item before shipping. We will be 100% responsible for the damaged goods if it's caused by our improper sewing or package.
What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?+
MOQ varies by product type. Running waist bags start from 100 pcs, hydration backpacks from 200 pcs. For new designs or complex customizations, MOQ may be higher. Contact us for specific requirements.
Can I get a sample before bulk order?+
Yes, samples are available upon request. Sample cost may be charged but will be refunded in your first bulk order. Sample lead time is 5-7 days.
What payment terms do you accept?+
We accept T/T (wire transfer), PayPal for small orders, and L/C for large orders. Typical terms: 30% deposit, 70% balance before shipment.
